*Introduction · Welcome*

My job title is Business Process Analyst. What that actually means is: I show up, ask uncomfortable questions, draw boxes and arrows until something makes sense, and then tell you why your current process is three steps too long and quietly bleeding money.

Ten years of this. Salesforce transformations, CPQ implementations, NPI launches, UAT cycles that tested my patience as much as the software. I bridge the gap between what the business wants and what actually gets built — and I push back hard when those two things need to be closer together.

> I don't just gather requirements — I challenge them, pressure-test them, and help teams build processes that actually scale.

## What I Actually Do

**End-to-end Salesforce** across the full lead-to-order lifecycle — lead conversion, deal management, CPQ, quote creation, and order processing.

**Process analysis** with a sharp lens on automation opportunities, ROI, and operational efficiency.

**New Product Introduction (NPI)** — analysing downstream impacts on CRM and external systems when new products go to market.

**Requirements documents, solution documents, process flows, and user stories** — the full documentation stack, built to give stakeholders a clear picture of trade-offs before a single dollar is spent.

CBAP certified. Which is a fancy way of saying I passed a very long exam about things I was already doing anyway.

## The Side Quest

These days I'm also deep in AI — and I mean properly deep, not just dabbling. I'm actively building my toolkit and figuring out how tools like Claude, Replit, Zapier, NotebookLM, Rovo, and Loom can fundamentally change the way analysts work. Not just automate the boring parts — but genuinely reimagine how analysis, documentation, and solution design get done.

I believe the most effective analysts in the next five years will be the ones who know how to work alongside AI. I'm not waiting for five years from now. I'm building that skillset today.

Tools I'm working with: **Claude · Replit · Zapier · NotebookLM · Rovo · Loom · Gemini · GitHub**
